The Solution

Live below your means & Make a budget

How can you possibly save money if you spend more than you earn? means having enough savings, financial investments, and cash on hand to afford the kind of life we desire for ourselves or to enable us to pursue the career we want without being driven by earning a set salary each year

Get rid of high-interest debt

Pay-off credit card and all other consumer debt that has an interest rate of more than 3% - 4%.

The average interest rate on a credit is about 20% - 25%. This means that you could pay up to $25 on each $100 that you don't pay off. If you owe $1000, you pay $250 just in interest.

Build your emergency fund

Emergencies happen, that's just apart of life. However, you don't want that to ruin your financial well-being.

A good, general rule-of-thumb of to keep about 6 - 8 months worth of expenses on hand in a savings account to cover any emergencies like losing a job

Increase your income

The fastest way to start moving toward financial freedom is to increase your income to add some padding from your expenses

However, don't give into lifestyle inflation. This is when you start spending more money since you started making more money. Avoid this at all costs.

Get your money to start making money for you

This is typically achieved by investing in the stock market but there are other avenues. You can only work so many hours in a day so the only way you can really get ahead of the curve is make money while you're not working
